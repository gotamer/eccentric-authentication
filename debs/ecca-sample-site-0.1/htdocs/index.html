Welcome to the Favorite Number Site

Welcome to the favorite number site. Here you can share your favorite number with the world.

Take a look at the current most favorite numbers.

Everyone can come and visit the site. Reading is open for everyone. Writing is limited to members only. Imagine the mess if everyone could write anything.

To become a member, all you have to do is register. You choose the username you wish to have and your browser must come up with some cryptographic bits to make it secure. With Firefox, that is easy. It does the all the difficult cryptographic work for you.

After registering, you can log in with the cryptographic bits and safely share your favorite number to the world.

Does your favorite number change over time, feel free to come back and change it. You could even update it like the weather. Just let your browser log you in with the cryptographic bits and update your number.

No joke

Well, a site to show off favorite numbers is a joke. What's not a joke is the technology behind the registration and log in pages. It's the worlds easiest Client Certificate system.

Its called Eccentric Authentication. For more information, see the Explanation page.
